About Traveloge

Hi! I'm Sethum Dinusha, a Mechanical Engineering Technologist studying at Uva Wellassa University.
Beyond engineering, I'm passionate about exploring new places and documenting my travel adventures. From mountain peaks to hidden beaches, I love discovering the beauty of Sri Lanka and sharing these experiences.
This blog is my journey - a collection of travel stories, 
tips, and the incredible moments I've captured along the way. Join me as I explore, learn, and grow through every adventure!
